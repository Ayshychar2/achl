Answers To Common Questions

Can colleges partner with ACHL?

Yes. We collaborate with colleges for certification programs, workshops, placements and industry sessions.

Can companies hire through ACHL?

Absolutely. Companies can submit hiring requirements and receive shortlisted candidates from our trained talent pool.

Do students receive placement support?

High-performing students may receive placement assistance through our hiring partners.
